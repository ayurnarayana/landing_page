What We Treat

Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy solutions for a wide range of conditions, delivered by certified professionals with consistent quality and care.

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Treatment for joint pain, arthritis, sports injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation, and musculoskeletal conditions.

  • Joint pain & stiffness
  • Sports injury recovery
  • Post-surgery rehabilitation
  • Arthritis management

Neurological Physiotherapy

Specialized care for stroke recovery, paralysis, Parkinson's disease, and other neurological conditions.

  • Stroke rehabilitation
  • Paralysis management
  • Parkinson's therapy
  • Balance & coordination

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Age-specific treatments focusing on mobility, strength, fall prevention, and maintaining independence in daily activities.

  • Fall prevention
  • Mobility enhancement
  • Strength training
  • Pain management

Cardiopulmonary Physiotherapy

Cardiac rehabilitation, and respiratory therapy for improved lung and heart function.

  • Cardiac rehabilitation
  • Breathing exercises
  • Post-COVID recovery
  • Respiratory therapy

Women's Health Physiotherapy

Pre and postnatal care, pelvic floor therapy, and condition-specific treatment for women's health concerns.

  • Prenatal & postnatal care
  • Pelvic Rehabilitation
  • Pregnancy-related pain
  • Core strengthening

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Developmental support for children with motor delays, cerebral palsy, and other pediatric conditions.

  • Developmental delays
  • Cerebral palsy care
  • Motor skill development
  • Pediatric Neurological Rehabilitation
