## COLOR GUIDELINES FOR IMAGES

To maintain brand consistency, images should align with these color palettes:

**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Green: #2D5F4D
- Gold Accent: #D4A574
- Cream/Neutral: #FAF8F5

**Image Tone Recommendations:**
- Warm, healing tones
- Natural lighting
- Professional but approachable
- Avoid harsh colors or stark contrasts
- Soft, calming atmosphere

---

## IMAGE OPTIMIZATION TIPS

1. **File Size:** Compress all images to under 500KB for web performance
2. **Format:** Use JPG for photos, PNG for logos/icons with transparency
3. **Resolution:** 72 DPI for web (double for retina displays)
4. **Naming:** Use lowercase, hyphens (no spaces or underscores)
5. **Alt Text:** Already included in HTML for accessibility
